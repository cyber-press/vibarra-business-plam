1. Executive Summary

Vibarra LLC is a Texas-based multi-member creative company focused on producing immersive cultural experiences that combine fashion, music, gaming, and urban community engagement. Our core mission is to provide premium events that bridge entertainment, lifestyle, and entrepreneurship, targeting Afro-urban Gen Z and Millennial audiences. Vibarra is founded by a team of four professionals with diverse expertise and a shared creative vision.

Our upcoming flagship event will be a hybrid fashion + gaming show hosted at a partner venue lounge in Texas, where we will collaborate with local talents, vendors, and creatives. Vibarra’s model leverages strategic collaborations and monetization through sponsorships, merch, and experiential campaigns.

3. Market Analysis

Target Demographics: Gen Z & Millennials (ages 18–35), Afro-urban youth, creatives, gamers, fashion enthusiasts, social influencers.

Industry Trends:

  • Growth in experiential events and lifestyle pop-ups
  • Esports and skill-based gaming as monetization streams
  • Social-first brand engagement (short-form video, viral campaigns)

Competitor Insight: Most Texas-based events focus on either fashion or nightlife. Vibarra’s hybrid event structure is positioned to stand out by integrating cultural showcases with competition, merch, and content.

4. Marketing & Promotion Strategy

  • Instagram + TikTok promo reels (teasers, behind-the-scenes)
  • Influencer marketing: curated guest invites with cross-posting
  • QR flyer drops in universities and youth hubs
  • Short-form email newsletter and WhatsApp blasts
  • Vibarra landing page & registration form for RSVPs

5. Operations Plan

Event Format: Hybrid show with gaming, performances, fashion runway, vendor booths

Frequency: Quarterly events (4x annually)

Roles:

  • Event logistics and schedule managed by Ladi
  • Vendor and budget coordination by Prince
  • Branding, stage visuals, and vibe by Press
  • Sponsor outreach and deals by Chris

6. Products & Services

  • Runway fashion showcases
  • Competitive gaming tournaments
  • Live DJ sets and cultural dance groups
  • Onsite merch + digital store
  • Collaborative content packages (for brands, vendors)
